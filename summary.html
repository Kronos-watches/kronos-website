Project summary

Kronos isn’t about tracking stress. It’s about responding to it.

The system is designed to deliver calm the moment stress is detected, with no manual input required.

Why it matters

Most wearables stop at awareness, leaving users to decide how to respond. Kronos removes decision fatigue by acting instantly.

  • Closed-loop response in real time
  • Minimal interface for daily wear
  • Automatic calm without interruption

Project highlights

  • Continuous monitoring with multi-signal verification
  • Automatic calm response via audio
  • Designed for students, professionals, and focused work

Judges takeaway

Kronos reframes wearables from passive tracking to active support, delivering calm as an automatic system response.

Project readiness

The concept balances affordability, premium design, and a clear user benefit with minimal cognitive load.
