Technical overview

Multi-sensor intelligence, simplified.

A closed-loop system that senses, verifies, and responds automatically.

Heart rate & HRV

Optical PPG sensor tracks cardiovascular changes in real time.

Skin conductance

GSR/EDA electrodes detect changes in stress response.

Motion detection

Accelerometer filters out stress spikes caused by activity.

Response output

Bluetooth speaker or headphones deliver calming audio instantly.

Kronos is not a medical or diagnostic device.

System logic

Closed-loop architecture

Kronos continuously senses stress signals, verifies them in parallel, and triggers a response without requiring user interaction.

Sensor layer

PPG + GSR + accelerometer with on-device preprocessing.

Decision layer

Multi-signal thresholding to reduce false positives.

Response layer

Immediate audio activation through connected devices.
