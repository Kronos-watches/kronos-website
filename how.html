How it works

From sensing to calm in seconds.

Kronos verifies stress across multiple signals, filters out activity spikes, and responds automatically with calming audio.

01

Senses Stress

Optical heart-rate sensors, HRV, and skin conductance continuously capture physiological stress signals throughout the day.

02

Detects Change

When multiple indicators rise above a threshold, Kronos detects elevated stress while filtering out movement and physical activity.

03

Responds Automatically

Calming audio activates instantly through a connected speaker or headphones—no apps, alerts, or manual input required.

Signal Fusion

Multi-signal verification

Kronos confirms elevated stress by blending heart rate, HRV, skin conductance, and motion patterns to reduce false positives caused by movement.

Stage 1

Baseline mapping

Establishes daily ranges for stress signals in context.

Stage 2

Cross-checking signals

Requires multiple signals to agree before triggering a response.

Stage 3

Automatic calm

Activates audio instantly, without distracting the user.
